Frequently Asked Questions

What's your booking process like?

+

My booking process begins with an initial consultation (either in person or virtually) to discuss your vision. Once we determine the details, a 50% deposit and signed contract secures your date. The remaining balance is due two weeks before your session.

How far in advance should I book?

+

For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak season dates. Portrait and boudoir sessions typically require 4-8 weeks advance booking, though I occasionally have last-minute availability.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

+

Absolutely! I'm available for travel within Southern California, across the US, and internationally. Travel fees vary by location. For destination weddings or sessions, let's discuss your needs during our consultation.

What if I need to reschedule?

+

Life happens! Rescheduling requests made more than 2 weeks before your session can be accommodated at no additional fee, subject to availability. Changes within 14 days may incur a rebooking fee. Please refer to your contract for specific terms.
