Common Questions

What to Expect

What should I wear to my session?

+

We'll provide a detailed style guide upon booking, but consider bringing 2-3 outfits that make you feel confident. This can include lingerie, oversized shirts, structured pieces, or meaningful personal items.

Do I need modeling experience?

+

Absolutely not! We provide complete posing guidance throughout your session. Our approach focuses on natural expressions and poses that complement your unique features.

How long does a session last?

+

Sessions typically last 2-4 hours, including hair and makeup. This allows plenty of time for outfit changes and ensuring you feel comfortable with each pose and setup.

When will I see my photos?

+

Your image reveal session will be scheduled approximately two weeks after your photoshoot. During this private session, you'll view your carefully edited collection and select your favorite images.
