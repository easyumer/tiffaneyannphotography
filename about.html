Hi! I'm Tiffaney!

As a queer photographer in Los Angeles, I've always been drawn to creating spaces where people can truly be themselves. My passion for photography began when I realized how powerful it is to see yourself authentically captured through someone else's lens.

The truth is, authentic connection invigorates me. You know that moment when you're with someone and they completely drop their guard? That beautiful, raw energy fills the air—and that's when magic happens.

That's what I believe every person deserves to experience during their photoshoot—that exciting feeling of being truly seen and celebrated exactly as you are. And if you, like me, crave that experience, let's chat and make it happen!

My Story

2010

The Beginning

My journey as a photographer began here, inspired by the lack of authentic representation I saw in mainstream media. As a queer woman, I wanted to create the images I wished I had seen growing up.

2015

Finding My Voice

After studying photography and working with several established studios in Los Angeles, I began developing my unique style—one focused on authenticity, emotion, and creating safe spaces for true self-expression.

2018

TiffaneyAnn Photography

I launched my own studio with a clear mission: to create a safe, empowering space for all clients—especially those who might feel overlooked or misunderstood in traditional photography settings.

"I don't just photograph people—I photograph their truths."
Now

Today

Today, I specialize in luxury portrait, wedding, and boudoir photography with an emphasis on authentic representation. My approach is deeply personal—I believe that truly spectacular images emerge when people feel completely comfortable being themselves in front of my lens.

Safe Space Commitment

As a queer photographer, I understand the importance of feeling completely comfortable and celebrated during your photography experience. My studio is a judgment-free zone where everyone is welcomed, respected, and encouraged to express their authentic selves.

01

Personalized Approach

Every session begins with a conversation about your comfort levels, boundaries, and vision. I'll never make assumptions about how you identify or what makes you feel celebrated.

02

Inclusive Language

I'm committed to using your preferred names, pronouns, and terminology. Clear, respectful communication is the foundation of every great photography experience.

03

Privacy & Consent

Your comfort and privacy are paramount. We'll discuss image usage and sharing permissions in detail, and you'll always have final say over how your images are used and shared.

04

Ongoing Education

I continuously educate myself on inclusive practices and diverse perspectives. My commitment to providing a safe space is an evolving practice that grows with each client I meet.
